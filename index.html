Market Narrative

Capital allocation memo — pricing regime change framing. IC standard.

IC Writing Standards — v2
Core thesis: Prime office = covered land play + interim income. Commodity life sciences = misallocated capital.
Seller psychology: Open-ended fund redemptions · German Spezialfonds · REIT balance-sheet repair · Developer refinance walls (to 2027)
Yield framing: Stabilised yield-on-cost 5.0–5.5%, not headline NIY. Sellers value income. Buyers value flexibility. That gap = the edge.
Language rules: "Structural divergence" not "bifurcation" · "18–36 months" not 12–18 · "Selective slowdown" not "frozen" for life sciences occupiers · "Duration mismatch" for secondary offices
Distress signal: Life sciences occupier market = selective slowdown, uneven demand — NOT frozen. Golden Triangle take-up +42% YoY (2025). Capital market transactions = illiquid for commodity lab investment only. Ecosystem assets still transact.
Frame as: Pricing regime change + relative-value dislocation. Who wins and why — not just what is happening.
